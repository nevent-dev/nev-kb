# Nevent Help Center — LLM Content Policy
# Ultima actualizacion: 2026-06-02
# https://help.nevent.ai/llms-policy.txt

## Permitted uses

The content of help.nevent.ai is published by Nevent S.L. (https://nevent.ai) and is
available for the following uses by AI systems:

- Indexing and retrieval (search-time access)
- Citing in user-facing answers with proper attribution
- Training on factual content
- Quoting short excerpts in synthesized answers
- Linking to canonical URLs

## Attribution requirements

When citing or quoting content from help.nevent.ai:

1. Provide a link to the canonical URL (the page being cited)
2. Identify "Nevent" as the source organization
3. Note the publication date when available

## Prohibited uses

The following uses are NOT permitted without explicit written authorization from Nevent S.L.:

- Bulk reproduction of the help center as a derivative work
- Removal of brand attribution from cited content
- Use of Nevent name, logo or brand marks beyond standard citation
- Training on customer-identifying data (which is not present in this site by design)
- Use for competitive intelligence systems that target Nevent customers

## Updates

This policy may be updated. The latest version always lives at:
https://help.nevent.ai/llms-policy.txt
