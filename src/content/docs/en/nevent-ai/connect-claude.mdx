In under 2 minutes you'll have Claude connected to your Nevent CRM. From there, you can ask about your campaigns, audiences, analytics and ads directly from the chat.

You don't need to install anything. Everything works from Claude.ai settings in your browser.

## Before you start

You need:
- An active [Claude.ai](https://claude.ai) account (Pro plan or higher — connectors are not available on the free plan)
- An active [Nevent](https://admin.nevent.es) account with administrator access

## Step 1: open Claude settings

Go to [claude.ai](https://claude.ai) and click your name or avatar in the bottom-left corner of the sidebar. A context menu opens.

{/* TODO: screenshot — Claude.ai sidebar with the user menu expanded. Highlight the "Settings" option. Save to /images/guias/nevent-ai/connect-claude/step-1.png */}

Select **Settings** in the menu.

## Step 2: go to the connectors section

Inside settings, look in the left menu for the **Connectors** or **Integrations** section, depending on your version of Claude.

{/* TODO: screenshot — Claude.ai settings panel with the Connectors section visible in the side menu. Save to /images/guias/nevent-ai/connect-claude/step-2.png */}

Click **Connectors**.

## Step 3: add a custom connector

In the connectors section you'll see any connectors you currently have set up (if this is your first time, it will be empty). Click the **Add custom connector** or **Add connector** button to add a new one.

{/* TODO: screenshot — Connectors page with the "Add custom connector" button visible. Save to /images/guias/nevent-ai/connect-claude/step-3.png */}

## Step 4: enter the Nevent AI URL

A field appears where you enter the server URL. Type this address exactly:

```
https://mcp.nevent.ai/mcp
```

{/* TODO: screenshot — form with the URL field and "https://mcp.nevent.ai/mcp" entered. Save to /images/guias/nevent-ai/connect-claude/step-4.png */}

Click **Save** or **Confirm** to save.

## Step 5: authorize access with your Nevent account

Claude will open a Nevent authorization window. You'll see the Nevent login screen.

{/* TODO: screenshot — OAuth authorization screen with the Nevent login form. Save to /images/guias/nevent-ai/connect-claude/step-5.png */}

Enter your Nevent Admin email and password and click **Sign in**. Nevent verifies your credentials and returns control to Claude.

:::note[Note]
This authorization uses OAuth 2.1, the same standard used by "Sign in with Google". Nevent never shares your password with Claude — it only issues a temporary session token.
:::

## Step 6: confirm the connector is active

After authorizing, you return to the connectors screen. The Nevent connector now appears in the list with the status **Connected**.

{/* TODO: screenshot — Connectors screen showing the Nevent connector with Connected status. Save to /images/guias/nevent-ai/connect-claude/step-6.png */}

That's it. The connector is active.

## Result: test the connection

Open a new conversation in Claude and type your first question:

```
List my last 5 email campaigns with their open rate
```

Claude will query your Nevent account and reply with real data in seconds.

{/* TODO: screenshot — Claude.ai chat with the question and a response showing a campaigns table. Save to /images/guias/nevent-ai/connect-claude/result.png */}

:::tip[Tip]
If Claude responds that it can't access Nevent data, verify that the connector shows as "Connected" in Settings → Connectors. If the problem persists, check the [frequently asked questions](/en/nevent-ai/faq).
:::
