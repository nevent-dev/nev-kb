When you connect Nevent AI to Claude or ChatGPT, you have access to virtually all the information and actions in your Nevent account — all from the chat window.

This section explains what you can do in each area. These are not example phrases — those are in [Ready-to-use prompts](/en/nevent-ai/ready-to-use-prompts/) — but the real scope of each capability: what queries you can make, what actions the assistant can prepare, and where the current limits are.

---

## Areas covered by Nevent AI

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Event analytics" icon="bars">
    Ask about sales, attendance, revenue and any metrics for your events. Filter by date, ticket type or segment. The assistant crosses data instantly.

    [View analytics →](/en/nevent-ai/what-you-can-do/analytics/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Campaigns" icon="rocket">
    Check the performance of your email, SMS and WhatsApp sends. Create drafts and schedule campaigns without opening the admin panel.

    [View campaigns →](/en/nevent-ai/what-you-can-do/campaigns/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Audience and segments" icon="list-format">
    Create contact segments with any behavioral, purchase or interaction criteria. Preview the size before saving.

    [View audiences →](/en/nevent-ai/what-you-can-do/audience/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email templates" icon="document">
    List, preview, clone and edit your templates. Send real test emails to your inbox before using them in a campaign.

    [View templates →](/en/nevent-ai/what-you-can-do/templates/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Paid advertising" icon="magnifier">
    Analyze the performance of your Meta, Google and TikTok campaigns. Compare ROAS, CPM, CTR and attribute ticket sales to each ad.

    [View advertising →](/en/nevent-ai/what-you-can-do/paid-media/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Link tracking" icon="open-book">
    Generate tracking links per user or per channel. Measure clicks, devices, countries and CTR for each campaign.

    [View tracking →](/en/nevent-ai/what-you-can-do/link-tracking/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Deliverability" icon="warning">
    Check the status of your sending domain, suppression rate and bounce reasons. Detect problems before they affect your campaigns.

    [View deliverability →](/en/nevent-ai/what-you-can-do/deliverability/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Multi-account management" icon="setting">
    If you manage more than one promoter or festival, switch accounts with a single instruction without signing out.

    [View multi-account →](/en/nevent-ai/what-you-can-do/multi-account/)
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## How to read each page

Each page in this section follows the same structure:

1. **What you can ask** — read-only queries available at all times.
2. **What the assistant can prepare for you** — actions available in "Read and prepare" or "Read, prepare and send" mode.
3. **A real example** — a concrete workflow with chained prompts.
4. **What it cannot do yet** — current limits so you're not caught off guard.
