This catalog collects single-step questions you can copy directly into Claude or ChatGPT with Nevent AI connected. These are one-shot prompts: you ask the question and get the answer.

If you're looking for a complete workflow — several chained steps toward a concrete goal — take a look at the [use cases](/en/nevent-ai/use-cases/). If you want to understand the full scope of what you can ask the assistant by area, visit the [What you can do](/en/nevent-ai/what-you-can-do/) section.

---

## Campaign analysis

When you want to know how your previous sends performed:

- "Which campaigns sent last month had an open rate above 30%?"
- "Show me the performance of the 'Summer Gala' campaign — opens, clicks and unsubscribes"
- "Compare the last three email campaigns by click rate"
- "How many bounces did we accumulate in February and what was the most common cause?"
- "Give me the full metrics for the Spring Festival, with an anomaly analysis"
- "How many people opened the last campaign more than once?"
- "Which day of the week has the best open rate in my last 10 campaigns?"

## Event and ticket analysis

To review the sales performance of a specific event:

- "Show me registrations for Festival X broken down by ticket type"
- "How many VIP tickets are still available for Saturday's concert?"
- "What was the total revenue from the 'Rock in the Park' event?"
- "List all attendees who bought a VIP ticket in 2025"
- "How many tickets were sold in the first week of sales for the festival?"
- "Which day of the week do I sell the most tickets for my events?"

:::tip[Tip]
If you're not sure what data you have available, ask: "What information do I have available in my account?" and the assistant will list all the data sources.
:::

## Creating and managing audience segments

To work with specific groups of contacts:

- "Create a segment with all attendees who bought a VIP ticket in 2025 but haven't opened any email in the last 90 days"
- "How many people are in the 'Active fans' segment?"
- "List all my segments with the number of contacts in each one"
- "Preview how many people would be in a segment of attendees from Madrid who have bought in the last 6 months"
- "Update the 'Early birds' segment to also include people who bought in the last 30 days"
- "What criteria are available for creating a segment?"

## Scheduling and creating campaigns

To prepare and launch a send:

- "Schedule the 'Summer Gala' campaign for next Monday at 10:00"
- "Create an email campaign draft for the 'Active fans' segment using the 'Festival announcement' template"
- "What status is the 'March newsletter' campaign in? Has it been sent yet?"
- "List all campaigns in draft status"
- "How many SMS campaigns were sent this quarter?"

:::caution[Important]
To schedule or create campaigns, Nevent AI needs to be in "Read and prepare" mode or higher. In "Read-only" mode you can view data but cannot modify anything. See the [permissions and security](/en/nevent-ai/permissions-and-security/) guide for more information.
:::

## Paid media and advertising

To analyze the performance of your paid campaigns on Meta, Google or TikTok:

- "Compare the ROAS of our Meta campaigns in March"
- "Which ad groups had the lowest CPM last week?"
- "How much did we spend on advertising in February and how many tickets can be attributed to those campaigns?"
- "Show me the daily CTR of the Summer Festival Instagram campaign"
- "Is the Meta Ads account connected and synced correctly?"
- "Which ad creative performed best last week?"
- "Compare spend and ROAS between my Meta and Google campaigns in April"

## Short URLs and link tracking

To measure the performance of the links you share:

- "Create individual tracking links for the 30 influencers in the 'Summer Gala' campaign"
- "How many clicks has the ticket sales link had this week?"
- "Show me the 10 users who generated the most clicks on the campaign links"
- "List all active short URLs with their click count"
- "Which countries are the clicks to the festival link coming from?"
- "What device do most people who click my links use?"

## Email deliverability

To make sure your emails reach the inbox:

- "Is my sending domain validated and configured correctly?"
- "How many suppressions have we accumulated this month and what are the main reasons?"
- "Is this month's bounce rate within normal limits?"
- "How many spam suppressions did we have in the last 30 days?"

:::note[Note]
A suppression rate above 2% is a warning signal. If you see this number, check the [deliverability](/en/nevent-ai/what-you-can-do/deliverability/) guide before your next send.
:::

## Email templates

To work with your templates before creating a campaign:

- "List all my email templates with how many times each has been used"
- "Show me a preview of the 'Purchase confirmation' template with data from a real contact"
- "Which templates have I used in the last 3 months?"
- "Clone the 'Festival announcement' template and rename it 'Festival announcement 2026'"
- "Send me a test of the 'Monthly newsletter' template to my email"

## Multi-account management

If you manage more than one account or promoter from Nevent:

- "Which accounts do I have available in my Nevent access?"
- "Switch to the 'Vigo Jazz Festival' promoter"
- "Go back to my main account"
