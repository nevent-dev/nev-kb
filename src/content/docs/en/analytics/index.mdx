# What you can measure with Nevent

With Nevent you can measure everything that happens in your live events business: how many tickets you sold, at what pace, which campaigns performed best, how much your fans are worth over time and whether your emails are actually reaching the inbox.

This section is the complete catalogue of available metrics, organised into 7 areas.

## How many metrics can you query?

Over 80 distinct metrics, grouped into 7 thematic areas. You can combine any metric with analysis dimensions (by date, by event, by channel, by fan segment) and apply time filters to look at specific periods or compare with a previous period.

## How do you access these metrics?

The same data is available through three different channels:

- **Nevent admin panel** — visual interface, no code required, with exportable charts and tables
- **AI conversation** — ask in plain English to Claude or ChatGPT via MCP
- **Direct API** — for developers who want to integrate the data into their own tools

## Data freshness

- Transaction data (purchases, accesses): latency under 5 minutes
- Aggregated metrics (by event, by period): updated daily
- Campaign and attribution metrics: updated several times per day
