# 6 use cases for measuring your marketing performance

Knowing your metrics by heart is useless if you do not know what questions to ask the data. This section brings together six real situations that repeat themselves in the day-to-day life of a promoter: from the monthly advertising report to long-term fan loyalty analysis.

Each use case describes the business question, how it is measured, what result to expect and when it makes most sense to apply it.

## What are these use cases for?

If you have data but do not know exactly what to query, these examples give you an actionable starting point. You can apply them directly in the Nevent admin panel, ask for them in plain language via the AI assistant or pass them to your team as a template for recurring analysis.
