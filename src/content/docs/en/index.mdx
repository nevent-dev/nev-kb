## Why use Nevent AI

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Live in 2 minutes" icon="approve-check">
    No installation, no local setup. Add `https://mcp.nevent.ai/mcp` as a connector in Claude or ChatGPT, authorize with your Nevent account, and you're done.
  </Card>
  <Card title="52 tools" icon="puzzle">
    9 categories: analytics, segmentation, campaigns, templates, deliverability, paid media (Meta · Google · TikTok), short URLs and multi-account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Your data, your account" icon="seti:lock">
    OAuth 2.1 authentication per user. Each session is isolated. Your data is not used to train models.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Three permission levels" icon="setting">
    Read-only (consult only), Read and prepare (consult + create drafts) or Read, prepare and send (live sends). You decide what the assistant can do.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Native multi-account" icon="document">
    If you manage multiple accounts, switch between them within the same conversation without re-authenticating.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Compatible with everything" icon="seti:plan">
    Claude.ai, ChatGPT, Claude Desktop, Claude Code, Cursor, Cline, Continue and VS Code.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
